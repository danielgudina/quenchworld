The Funky Orange smoothie

The Amazing funky Orange features a fruity blast of flavor with an orange kick that just won't quit.

  • 12 oz. orange juice
  • 2 scoops orange sherbet
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 Tablespoons honey
  • 1 scoop ice
  • Mix all ingredients in a blender.

The Melon smoothie

This pink drink is the grown up equivalent of zipping down a Slip-n-slide or drinking cold water right from the hose on the hottest day as a kid

  • Preparation time: 5 min
  • 1/4 cantaloupe - peeled, seeded and cubed
  • 1/4 honeydew melon - peeled, seeded and cubed.
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • in a blender, combine cantaloupe, honeydew, lime juice and sugar
  • Blend until smoooth, pour glasses and serve.

The raspberry Smoothie

10 minutes to prepare this delicious smoothie that will leave you craving more.

  • Prep time: 10 min
  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 container vanilla yoghurt
  • 1 Tablespoons honey
  • 4 ice cubes
  • Place banana, blackberries, raspberries, yoghurt, honey and icecubes into a blender. Blend until smooth

The Strawberry Smoothie

The Strawberry Smoothie is deliciously thick, perfectly sweet and bursting with fresh flavor.

  • 1 heaping cup or frozen strawberries
  • 1 medium ripe banana or 1 cup banana slices
  • Half of a medium orange, peeled and quartered
  • 3/4 cup to 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Add strawberries, banana, orange, milk and vanila extract to a blender
  • Blend until creamy and smooth. If its too thick, add a bit more milk.

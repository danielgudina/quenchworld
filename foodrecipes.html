The Spaghetti Aglio e olio

10 minutes to prepare this classic dish. The key is slowly toasting the garlic slices to a perfect golden brown.

  • 1 pound uncooked spaghetti and a 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 6 thinly sliced cloves garlic and 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh italian parsley and 1 cup finely grated Parmigiano- Reggiano cheese.
  • Boil a pot of lightly salted water. Cook spaghetti in the water and stir and drain to a pasta bowl.
  • When pasta is cooking, combine olive oil and garlic in a cold skillet. Cook over medium heat until garlic is golden brown. Stir red pepper flakes, salt and black pepper into pasta and pour in the garlic and hot olive oil.
  • Serve pasta topped with the cheese

The Pasta Melanzana

A finger licking delicacy that is just nutritiously good.

  • 3/4 cup bow tie (farfalle) pasta with 1 medium eggplant
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil and 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped with 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 cups fresh chopped spinach and 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • salt and pepper, cracked black pepper and 3/4 cup grated Parmesian cheese.
  • Boil a pot of lightly salted water then add pasta and cook for about 8 min utes and drain.
  • Meanwhile heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add garlic, cook and stir. Mix in eggplant and let it cook for 5 minutes without stirring. Then stir and cook for 5 min more.
  • Mix in spinach and season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally fpor 3 min. Stir in drained pasta and lemon juice along with 1/2 cup of Parmesian cheese. Transfer to a serving dish and top with remaining cheese and cracked black pepper.

The Roasted Veggie pasta

30 minutes to cook this wonderful rustic italian style pasta dish

  • ¼ pound fresh asparagus and 2 red bell pepper, sliced ¼ pound crimini mushrooms, sliced
  • 10 cloves roasted garlic, chopped and ½ tomato, quartered
  • ½ teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary and ½ teaspoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil and 8 ounces dry fettuccini noodles
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese and 2 tablespoons tapenade
  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Prepare asparagus by trimming woody base and cutting diagonally into 4 inch pieces
  • In a roasting pan, combine asparagus, bell pepper, mushrooms, roasted garlic and tomato. Sprinkle with rosemary and oregano, then drizzle with olive oil. Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes.
  • Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain. Toss with Parmesan cheese, tapenade and roasted vegetables.
